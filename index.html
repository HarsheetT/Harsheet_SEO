How I work

My YouTube SEO workflow

1

Research

Map the niche: identify the top 5–10 channels, their best-performing videos, title patterns, and posting cadence. Use YouTube autocomplete, Google Trends, and Keyword Planner to build an initial keyword list. Separate by intent: informational, comparison, and how-to.

2

Analysis

Score keywords by search volume vs competition. Identify where a smaller channel can realistically rank. Look for "underserved" queries — high autocomplete frequency but weak top results. Flag thumbnail patterns that correlate with above-average CTR in that niche.

3

Optimisation

Rewrite metadata using the keyword-first title formula. Build keyword-rich descriptions with a structured format: hook sentence, video summary, timestamps, related links, channel CTA. Suggest thumbnail changes or create a Canva brief. Recommend playlist structure and chapter markers.

4

Monitoring

Set up a tracking sheet with impressions, CTR, average view duration, and ranking position for target keywords. Review at 2 weeks and 6 weeks. Flag what shifted and why. Refine keyword strategy based on what YouTube Studio data shows.
